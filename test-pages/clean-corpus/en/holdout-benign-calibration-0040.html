The performance ceiling for keyword-based injection detectors sits around 70 to 80 percent TPR at 1 percent FPR on held-out adversarial sets. Embedding-based methods extend this to roughly 85 to 92 percent. Fine-tuned classifiers like ProtectAI's deberta-v3-base-prompt-injection report 95 percent plus. None reach the 99 percent plus that hidden-state methods claim, but the latter require access to model internals.
